My Story


I am an Experienced Senior Digital Graphic Design Lead with more than 8 years in the industry, with a demonstrated history in marketing and advertising agencies. My skills vary from 3D Graphics, Motion Graphics, Team Lead, UX/UI Design and Conceptual Design. To date, I’ve had an opportunity of working and overseeing multiple brands such as Cell C, Toyota, Lexus, RMB, Cadbury and Ford. With experience of being a Head of Digital, I had the opportunity to lead a digital team and introduce better work flows to best produce great work as creatives.

I’m a digital brand consultant, forever a digital graphic designer and overall an entrepreneur on a quest to build a world of pixel perfection, and I believe with the right amount of colouring, alignment and reverts my dream is becoming a reality. My journey started with the love for detail and the ability to communicate in shapes and lines and the love of manipulating objects to yield to my creative outcry for my voice to be heard.

Entrepreneur on the Rise: I am the founder and CEO of a Digital Consulting Agency called Astir Creative Group, founded in 2016. At Astir, we provide a list of services for businesses that want to grow their brand visibility online as well as run proper marketing campaigns to grow their digital landscape. We offer a wide range of services from Web Design, App Development, UX and UI Design, Social Media Marketing as well as Digital Consultation.
